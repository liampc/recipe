Pineapple Chicken

Description

An easy weeknight dinner, this quick chicken stir-fry with pineapple is as delicious as it is colorful!
Serve with rice for a complete meal, or fried rice for a treat. Add red chile flakes to the mix if you want something sweet and spicy!
